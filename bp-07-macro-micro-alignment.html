Blueprint 07

Macro–Micro Healthcare Alignment Model

A blueprint that eliminates the historical disconnect between national policy and bedside reality by creating data-driven alignment between strategy, regulation, and clinical workflows.

Extended Architecture Overview

The Macro–Micro Alignment Model ensures that high-level policies, incentives, and reforms translate directly into executable workflows inside hospitals, labs, radiology units, and care networks. This architecture prevents policy drift, operational chaos, and strategic fragmentation.

Alignment Primitives

  • Data Gravity Mapping
  • Policy-to-Action Compilation Layer
  • Workflow Constraint Injection
  • Role-Based Execution Channels
  • Bidirectional Feedback Loops

Dependencies

Upstream: 12-Layer AI Governance Model, Health Nation OS
Downstream: Workforce Orchestration, Clinical Flow Engines, Hospital Control Loop
