Blueprint 07 · Alignment Model

Macro–Micro Alignment Model

A national architecture ensuring that strategic priorities (macro) translate directly into real-world clinical, operational, and financial workflows (micro) without distortion, delay, or institutional drift.

Extended Architecture Overview

The Macro–Micro Alignment Model addresses the chronic disconnect between national strategies and real-world execution inside hospitals, clinics, and care networks. Without alignment, even well-designed policies fail at the point of care.

Core Components

  • Strategic → operational translation mechanisms
  • Cross-layer decision synchronization
  • Feedback loops for continuous correction
  • Organizational flow mapping
  • Integration with National OS governance pathways

Dependencies

Upstream: AI Governance Model, National Strategy Architecture
Downstream: Workforce Orchestration, Capacity Forecasting, National Digital Twin
