Population Health Intelligence Engine

BP-20 — National Epidemiological & Wellness Forecasting Layer

Executive Summary

BP-20 defines a sovereign-scale intelligence engine capable of predicting population health futures, modeling risk trajectories, identifying emerging disease clusters, and guiding proactive public health interventions at national scale.

Core Functions

  • National disease progression forecasting
  • Population-level risk stratification & segmentation
  • Behavioral, environmental, and genomic signals fusion
  • Predictive intervention planning
  • Epidemiological anomaly detection

System Architecture

  1. Data Fusion Layer — EHR, claims, mobility, climate, socioeconomic factors.
  2. Long-Horizon Forecast Models — multi-year simulation & risk estimation.
  3. Population Segmentation Engine — high-granularity clusters.
  4. Intervention Optimizer — allocates resources for maximum impact.
