12-Layer AI Governance Model

A complete national governance architecture defining how AI is developed, deployed, monitored, constrained, audited, and ethically aligned across the healthcare ecosystem.

Extended Architecture Overview

The 12-Layer AI Governance Model provides an end-to-end governance structure ensuring that AI systems across the health nation remain transparent, auditable, safe, clinically aligned, and socially legitimate. It defines policy, assurance, oversight, validation, safety monitoring, red-teaming, and ethical guardrails.

The 12 Layers

  • National AI Policy & Legislative Alignment
  • Ethical & Societal Guardrails
  • Model Registration & Traceability
  • Clinical Validation & Bias Auditing
  • Real-Time Monitoring & Drift Detection
  • Explainability & Transparency Layer
  • Risk Scoring & Safety Thresholds
  • Incident Response Protocols
  • AI Lifecycle Management
  • Human-in-the-Loop Governance
  • Security & Zero-Trust Enforcement
  • National Oversight Council Interface

Dependencies

Upstream: National Policy & Strategy
Downstream: Federated Clinical Reasoning Engine, Trust Grid, Compliance OS

Questions & Notes

Governments or institutions may request the full 12-layer governance documentation and audit templates.
