Cognitive Digital Front Door

BP-19 — National Intelligent Access Layer

Executive Summary

BP-19 establishes the national AI-powered entry point to the healthcare system. It transforms unstructured demand into structured clinical pathways through intelligent triage, intent detection, multimodal reasoning, and cross-institution routing.

Core Functions

  • AI-driven pre-triage using symptoms, text, voice, and images
  • Demand forecasting for hospitals and emergency services
  • Autonomous clinical routing to the right provider
  • Unified patient journey orchestration across institutions
  • Real-time risk scoring for escalation or de-escalation

System Architecture

  1. Intent Recognition Engine — NLP, vision, structured data parsing.
  2. Symptom Reasoning Models — probabilistic and rule-based hybrid logic.
  3. Routing & Allocation Layer — matches demand to national capacity grids.
  4. Continuous Learning Loop — improves personalization and accuracy.
