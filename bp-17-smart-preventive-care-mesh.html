Smart Preventive Care Mesh

BP-17 — National Preventive Intelligence Layer

Executive Summary

This blueprint defines a national-scale preventive intelligence mesh designed to predict disease trajectories, detect risk patterns early, and coordinate AI-enabled interventions across a population. By integrating behavioral, clinical, environmental, and socioeconomic datasets, the Preventive Care Mesh elevates health systems from reactive treatment models to proactive, continuous, and personalized prevention architectures.
