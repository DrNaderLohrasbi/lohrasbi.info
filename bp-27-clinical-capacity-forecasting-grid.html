Blueprint 27 · Capacity Forecasting

Clinical Capacity Forecasting Grid

A nationwide predictive intelligence grid that models real-time clinical capacity, anticipates overload, and orchestrates proactive allocation across hospitals, diagnostics, emergency care, and distributed networks.

Extended Architecture Overview

This blueprint builds a forecasting mesh that continuously measures and predicts strain across clinical infrastructure. By integrating patient inflow trends, resource elasticity, acuity patterns, and diagnostic throughput, the grid creates an anticipatory map of national clinical readiness.

Core Components

  • National Capacity Elasticity Engine
  • Cross-Institutional Forecast Mesh
  • Real-Time Acuity and Demand Prediction
  • Proactive Allocation Governance Layer
  • Multi-Domain Supply–Demand Reconciliation

Dependencies

Upstream: Patient Journey Engine, Dynamic Waiting Room System
Downstream: National Mission Control, Recovery Optimization Networks
