Blueprint 12 · Personal Intelligence Layer

Bespoke AI Assistant

A personalized, continuously learning AI layer tailored to each clinician, policymaker, and administrator — creating precision-grade augmentation across all national health workflows.

Extended Architecture Overview

The Bespoke AI Assistant adapts to individual cognitive patterns, behavioral preferences, decision styles, and domain expertise. It builds a longitudinal memory of interactions and provides anticipatory guidance across clinical, operational, and strategic domains.

Core Components

  • Adaptive cognitive profiling engine
  • Personalized decision-support chains
  • Real-time workflow augmentation
  • Memory continuity and long-term preference learning
  • Secure identity-bound AI instance per user

Dependencies

Upstream: Artificial Soul Framework, AI Governance Model
Downstream: Clinical Reasoning Engines, Multi-Agent Workflow AI
