Blueprint 02 · Doctrine

Architecture Over Repair

A national-level doctrine rejecting incremental fixes and promoting foundational redesign of healthcare systems through systemic, architectural thinking.

Extended Architecture Overview

Architecture Over Repair establishes a transformational principle: healthcare systems cannot be “patched” into efficiency. They must be redesigned at the structural, operational, informational, and AI-governance layers. This blueprint defines when, why, and how a nation transitions from repair-based thinking into architectural reconstruction.

Structural Components

  • Diagnosis of systemic failure modes
  • Redesign triggers and architectural thresholds
  • Lifecycle replacement models
  • Governance mechanisms for architectural transformation
  • Integration with the Health Nation OS core

Dependencies

Upstream: National Strategy Models, Crisis Architecture Framework
Downstream: Unified Governance Layer, Distributed Care Infrastructure
