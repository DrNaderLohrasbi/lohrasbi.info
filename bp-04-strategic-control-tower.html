Blueprint 04 · Command Layer

Strategic Control Tower

A national command architecture that consolidates intelligence, orchestrates priorities, and governs system-wide flow across Health Nation OS.

Extended Architecture Overview

The Strategic Control Tower serves as the central command organ of a national health system. It processes real-time signals, synthesizes intelligence across domains, and generates coordinated directives for hospitals, emergency networks, logistics, and national governance.

Core Components

  • Unified national decision engine
  • Priority orchestration logic
  • Real-time situational awareness grid
  • Multi-agency command synchronization
  • Integration with Crisis Architecture & Health Nation OS

Dependencies

Upstream: Crisis Architecture Framework
Downstream: Mission Control, National AI Governance Layer
