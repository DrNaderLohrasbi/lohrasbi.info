Blueprint 17 · National Simulation Layer

National Digital Twin for Healthcare

A real-time, nation-scale simulation architecture enabling predictive modeling, crisis rehearsal, capacity forecasting, and adaptive system governance across the full healthcare ecosystem.

Extended Architecture Overview

The National Digital Twin (NDT-H) creates a continuously updated virtual replica of the entire healthcare system — hospitals, clinics, logistics, workforce, supply chains, emergency networks, financial flows, and patient journeys. This environment enables proactive system steering, scenario testing, and architectural validation without risk to the real system.

Core Components

  • Real-time system state ingestion
  • Dynamic simulation engine
  • Crisis rehearsal & scenario planning
  • Capacity and load forecasting
  • Architecture validation & refinement loop

Dependencies

Upstream: Predictive Patient Journey Engine, Federated Reasoning Systems
Downstream: Autonomous Control Loops, National Resilience Models
